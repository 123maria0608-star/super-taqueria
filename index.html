Our Story

About Super Taqueria

Super Taqueria opened its doors in San Jose, CA with one goal: to create the kind of café you actually want to stay in. The kind with a great playlist, baristas who remember your order, and coffee that makes you close your eyes for a second. We partner with independent roasters, bake in-house daily, and keep things honest — because great coffee doesn't need to be complicated.
